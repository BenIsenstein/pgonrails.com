## Managing Your Account

### Creating a CRM Account

To create a new account with Ælen CRM:

1. Go to our **Sign Up** page.
2. Enter your business email, company name, and choose a workspace name.
3. Create a secure password.
4. Verify your email address.
5. Set up your workspace and invite teammates.

You’ll receive a confirmation email with instructions to get started.

### Updating Profile Information

To edit your personal details:

1. Log in to your account.
2. Click your profile avatar and navigate to **Settings > Profile**.
3. Update your name, job title, or contact information.
4. Save changes.

Some updates may require re-authentication for security purposes.

### Changing Your Password

If you’ve forgotten or want to change your password:

1. Use the **Forgot Password** link on the login screen to reset it.
2. If you’re logged in, go to **Settings > Security** to update your password manually.
3. Enable **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** for enhanced protection.

### Managing Account and Team Settings

CRM admins can customize workspace access and preferences:

- Invite or remove team members.
- Assign user roles (Admin, Sales, Support, etc.).
- Configure team-wide settings and feature access under **Settings > Team Management**.
- Adjust notification preferences and integration settings.

### Closing Your Account

To deactivate your CRM account:

1. Make sure all active tasks or leads are reassigned.
2. Export any critical data.
3. Contact Ælen CRM support with your request.

Note: Account closures are permanent and may require identity verification.
